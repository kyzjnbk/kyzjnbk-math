# Special Functions

## Hyperbolic Functions

### sinh

$$ \sinh z\equiv \frac{1}{2} (e^z-e^{-z}) $$

### cosh

$$ \cosh z \equiv \frac{1}{2}(e^z+e^{-z}) $$

### tanh


\begin{align}
\tanh (x) &= \frac{\sinh (x)}{\cosh (x)} \\
\frac{\partial \tanh (x)}{\partial x} &= \text{sech}^2(x) \\
\int \tanh (x) \, dx &= \log (\cosh (x))
\end{align}
